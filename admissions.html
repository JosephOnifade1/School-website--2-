Admissions

Welcome to the admissions section of our school! We are excited that you are considering joining our community.

Admission Process

To apply for admission, follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents, including transcripts and recommendation letters.
  3. Attend an admissions interview, if required.
  4. Wait for the admission decision.

Admission Requirements

Our admission requirements include:

  • Completed application form.
  • Copies of academic transcripts from the last two years.
  • Two recommendation letters.
